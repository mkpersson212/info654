What is a Meme?

In the digital age, memes evolved into viral images, videos, phrases, or formats shared widely online, often with humorous or satirical intent. Early internet memes were simple and text-based, but they quickly grew into a rich visual language that spans generations and platforms.

In my personal experience, sharing memes is a way of keeping in touch, albeit minimally, with friends that have shared experiences or ways of thinking, and remind them you're thinking of them.
